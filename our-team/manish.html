Manish Maheshwari

Manish is a seasoned tech executive, entrepreneur. previously served as Twitter India's Country Head, driving rapid user and revenue growth. Before Twitter, he was CEO of Network18 Digital, scaling its digital footprint to 100+ million users. Manish was elected to the Governing Council of Internet and Mobile Association of India (IAMAI), India's top digital policy body, where he also served as Treasurer. With an MBA (Honors) from The Wharton School, backed by a Ford Research Fellowship, he began his career at McKinsey & Company in New York.
